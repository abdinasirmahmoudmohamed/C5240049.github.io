Help Center

Navigating the Website

Our website has five main pages:

  • Home: Overview of our products and services
  • Products: View our product catalog and compare specifications
  • Book Consultation: Schedule a meeting with our experts
  • Help: This page with guidance
  • About: Information about our business

Using the Product Gallery

The Products page shows our available items in different categories:

  1. Browse through the product images
  2. Hover over images to see brief details
  3. Use the comparison table to evaluate different models
  4. Click manufacturer links for more technical details

Booking a Consultation

To book a product consultation:

  1. Fill in your personal information
  2. Select the product category you're interested in
  3. Choose your preferred contact method
  4. Select any accessories you might need
  5. Pick a convenient date
  6. Add any special requests in the comments
  7. Click "Submit Booking Request"

Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*) or will show an error if left blank.
